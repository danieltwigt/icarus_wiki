Troubleshooting
===============

This page contains general troubleshooting information related to ICARUS:

.. list-table::
  :width: 100 %
  :widths: 50 50
  :header-rows: 1

  * - Issue
    - Solution
  * - The VR headset display stays black
    - Reconnect the cable connecting the VR headset to the PC powering the headset. If needed, restart ICARUS afterwards.
  * - The VR headset refresh rate seems low
    - (Re)start the headset and leave this on for some minutes before retrying
  * - The VR scene turned into a mess
    - Restart the training level in VR  

|

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
